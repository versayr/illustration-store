Lindsay Blevins is an illustrator specializing in children's illustration and surface design. She received her BFA in Illustration from Lesley University College of Art and Design in Cambridge, Massachusetts. She grew up in Pennsylvania with a guinea pig named Fluffy and a rooster named Mr. Chickadee. She now resides in Boston, and has 8 chickens named Daisy, Petunia, Lavender, Parsley, Buttercup, Daffodil, Beansprout and Radish back at home in PA. She is constantly inspired by birds, gardens, animals, and fabric, and loves to spend her days sewing, painting, petting dogs, and reading picture books.

Lindsay currently sells her greeting cards at the Brookline Booksmith in Brookline, MA, and Porter Square Books in Cambridge, MA. Her work has also been included in Stew Magazine, a magazine for curious kids.
